Who I Am

My name is Guillemette Schroepfer and I am a Biomedical Engineering student double majoring in Religious Studies. I am passionate about inclusive design and ethical tech, and I am excited to explore these themes in this course. In my free time I enjoy running, spending time outdoors exploring new places, and trying new recipes.

What I Care About

Through my study of relgious studies, I have developed a strong interest in the ethical implications of technology and how it can be designed to be more inclusive and equitable. I am particularly interested in how technology is often developed knowing that it will be used by a diverse range of people, but without fully considering the needs and experiences of those users. For religious people in particular, additions Electric Medical Records (EMRs) have been implimented to include a patients religious affiliation, but often these lists are not inclusive of all religions and can having their religious needs overlooked.
